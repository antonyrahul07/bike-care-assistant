# 🏍️ Bike Care & Selection Assistant
🌐 **Live Demo:** [Click here](https://antonyrahul07.github.io/bike-care-assistant)

An AI-powered assistant that helps you choose the right bike 
and diagnose bike problems — without visiting a mechanic every time.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🤖 Bike Selection Assistant
- Chat with AI to find the perfect bike based on your needs
- Considers budget, usage (city/highway/off-road), and preferences
- Compares multiple bike models intelligently
- Saves time and confusion when buying a new bike

### 🔧 AI Mechanic Assistant
- Describe your bike problem in plain language
- Get instant diagnosis and repair guidance
- Save money by fixing minor issues yourself
- Available 24/7 — no appointment needed

---

## 💡 Problem It Solves

- ❌ Confused about which bike to buy? → ✅ AI recommends the best fit
- ❌ Minor bike issue? Don't want to visit mechanic? → ✅ AI diagnoses it instantly
- ❌ Wasting money on unnecessary mechanic visits → ✅ Fix it yourself with AI guidance
